# Scene 4C: Too Deep

You hesitate, but not long enough. 

The water grows darker faster than expected. The pressure feels heavier, and your surroundings blur slightly as you push past your comfort zone.

Your PDA flashes:

> "Warning: Oxygen low."

Then...

A sudden movement below. Fast! 

Much faster than anything you've seen so far.

You dont see the creature clearly... just a massive moving shape rushing upward before it veers off into the darkness. 

Silence returns, except for your pounding heartbeat. 

What do you do?
