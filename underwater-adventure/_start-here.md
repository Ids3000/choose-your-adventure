
# Underwater Adventure - (Subnautica)

## 🎬 Opening Crawl

### A NEW BEGINNING

You sit at the edge of the life pod, legs dangling just above the water. The surface moves gently below you, clear enough to see the reef stretching out beneath.

Small fish flicker through the coral, and sunlight ripples across the sand.

Your PDA hums softly:
> 💬 “Survival mode engaged. Environment stable."

The pod creaks behind you, reminding you how alone you are out here.

You take a breath and steady yourself.

The water looks calm. Inviting, even.

You lean forward slightly, watching your reflection distort across the surface.
